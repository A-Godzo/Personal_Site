My Hobbies

A glimpse into what brings me joy beyond work and study.

Video games have always been more than just a pastime for me—they're a dynamic hobby that blends challenge, creativity, and immersion. I particularly enjoy competitive first-person shooters like Call of Duty, Battlefield, and Crysis, where fast reflexes and tactical thinking make every match feel fresh and intense. On the other hand, I also love losing myself in open-world RPGs like The Witcher 3, Ghost Recon, and GTA, where rich storytelling and vast, explorable worlds offer a different kind of escape. Whether it's the adrenaline of a firefight or the satisfaction of completing a complex quest, gaming gives me a sense of focus, achievement, and fun that few other hobbies can match.

Movies are one of my favorite ways to unwind and get pulled into gripping stories and high-energy scenes. I’m especially drawn to action and thriller films—the kind that keep you on the edge of your seat and hit with intense pacing and clever twists. Some of my top picks include Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels and Snatch for their sharp dialogue and gritty style, as well as the Mission: Impossible series, which never fails to deliver jaw-dropping stunts and suspense. For me, a great movie is like a quick dive into another world, and that’s exactly what these films offer.
